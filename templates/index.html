About JONGRO and EINS

Jongro Academy started off as a leading Chinese educational institution preparing students with speaking abilities and also the HSK exam. After our success, we started our English department, EINS. Visit our blog for more information on this.

Our website will serve to show students their work progress online. Log in to check how you are doing in your classes! And with our Exam Online Platform you can take mock exams on our website and keep track of your test scores.

We will constantly update mock exams so that you can control your working schedule and so our teachers can give you feedback right away more efficiently.
